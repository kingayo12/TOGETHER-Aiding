    TOGETHER Aiding Aged AND TEENAGE MOTHERS Foundation, is an NGO dedicated to supporting the elderly who are destitute and the most vulnerable teenage mothers, we strive to provide a safe and supportive platform for these two delicate and often marginalized groups. Our mission is to empower and uplift these individuals by addressing their unique needs and providing them with resources and support to improve their quality of life.
