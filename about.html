About Us

About us

Give a helping hand to a needy people

TOGETHER Aiding Aged AND TEENAGE MOTHERS Foundation, is an NGO dedicated to supporting the elderly who are destitute and the most vulnerable teenage mothers, we strive to provide a safe and supportive platform for these two delicate and often marginalized groups. Our mission is to empower and uplift these individuals by addressing their unique needs and providing them with resources and support to improve their quality of life.
The elderly who are destitute and teenage mothers face significant challenges and are often overlooked by society. We believe that these individuals deserve to live with dignity and have access to the necessary support and resources to thrive. Our NGO was established to fill this crucial gap and provide a platform for these vulnerable populations to receive the care and assistance they deserve.
Our core values revolve around compassion, empathy, and advocacy. We believe in treating all individuals with respect and dignity, regardless of their age or circumstances. We are committed to advocating for the rights and well-being of the elderly who are destitute and teenage mothers, and we work tirelessly to ensure they have access to the resources and support they need to live fulfilling and meaningful lives.
Our vision is a world where every elderly person and teenage mother is valued, respected, and supported. We strive to create a community where these individuals can thrive and access the care and resources they need to lead happy and healthy lives. Through our work, we aim to break down barriers and create a more inclusive and compassionate society for all.

Who We Are

Our Values & Strategic Objectives

Our NGO is dedicated to supporting the elderly and teenage mothers by providing essential services, empowerment programs, and advocacy for a better future.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We value empathy and understanding towards the challenges faced by the elderly and teenage mothers.
  • Respect: We believe in treating all individuals with dignity and respect, regardless of their age or circumstances.
  • Empowerment: We strive to empower the elderly and teenage mothers to take control of their lives and make informed decisions for their well-being.
  • Advocacy: We are committed to advocating for the rights and needs of the elderly and teenage mothers, ensuring their voices are heard and their concerns addressed.
  • Collaboration: We believe in working together with partners, communities, and stakeholders to create holistic solutions for the elderly and teenage mothers.

Our Strategic Objectives

  • Provide Essential Services: Ensure access to basic needs such as healthcare, shelter, food, and clothing for the elderly and teenage mothers.
  • Empowerment Programs: Develop programs that promote self-sufficiency, skill-building, and financial literacy among the elderly and teenage mothers.
  • Advocacy & Awareness: Raise awareness about the unique challenges faced by the elderly and teenage mothers in society and advocate for policy changes to better support them.
  • Community Engagement: Engage with communities to create a supportive and inclusive environment for the elderly and teenage mothers.
  • Sustainable Support: Establish sustainable funding mechanisms and partnerships to ensure continuous support for the elderly and teenage mothers in the long term.
