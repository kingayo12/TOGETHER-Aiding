Welcome to TOGETHER Aiding Aged AND TEENAGE MOTHERS

A global non-profit organization commited to the wellbeing, dignity, advocacy and awareness of the Elderly and teenage mothers.

We are committed to bring dynamic change for the elderly by enhancing physical and mental quality of life, promoting social participation and integration,healthy aging, economic security, the and inclusion of older men and women in the societal change and development.
